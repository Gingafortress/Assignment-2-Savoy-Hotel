Rooms

Standard Room

Our beautifully renovated Standard rooms are a tranquil retreat from the hustle and bustle of the busy cities. The new elegant decor includes, pillow top mattresses, comfortable chairs and remote control TVs. Other in-room features include floor to ceiling, functioning windows, internet access and in-room safes. Our beautifully renovated marble bathrooms include luxurious bath products and blow dryers. Coffee/Tea makers and robes are available upon request.

Superior Room

Our elegant Superior rooms are newly renovated and located on floors 28 and higher. The new decor features stylish platform beds with pillow top mattresses, leather club chairs and remote control flat-screen TVs. Each room is ready for business with a spacious desk featuring internet access, reading lamp and speaker phone with dual line direct dial phones with message alert and voicemail in 4 languages. Other in-room features include floor to ceiling, functioning windows and in-room safes. Coffee/Tea makers and robes are available upon request.

Penthouse Suite

Our one-bedroom penthouse suites are a well deserved escape from the lights and action of the large cities. Located on the 50th to the 52nd floors high above the city rush, our suites have living rooms with stylish leather couches, club chairs and remote control flat screen TVs. The bedrooms have all of the amenities and luxury of our Superior rooms including new stylish platform beds with pillow top mattresses, leather club chairs and remote control flat screen TVs. Each room is ready for business with a spacious desk featuring internet access, reading lamp and speaker phone with dual line direct dial phones with message alert and voicemail in 4 languages. Other in-room features include floor to ceiling functioning windows and in-room safes. Coffee/Tea makers and robes are standard in all suites.
